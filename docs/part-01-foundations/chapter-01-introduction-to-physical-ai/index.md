# Chapter 1: Introduction to Physical AI

Welcome to the beginning of your journey into Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics! This chapter establishes the conceptual foundation you'll build upon throughout the course.

In the digital age, AI has transformed software‚Äîpowering search engines, recommendation systems, and conversational agents. But a profound shift is underway: **AI is breaking free from screens and entering the physical world.** This chapter explores what makes Physical AI fundamentally different from software-only systems and why humanoid robotics represents the pinnacle of this evolution.

## What You'll Learn

In this chapter, you will:

- **Understand the Digital-to-Physical Transition**: Grasp why embodied AI requires entirely new approaches beyond traditional software systems
- **Map the Robotics Revolution**: Identify key players, technologies, and market trends driving the Physical AI industry
- **Define Embodied Intelligence**: Understand the philosophical and technical foundations of robots that perceive, reason, and act in the real world
- **Survey Real-World Applications**: Explore current and emerging use cases across manufacturing, logistics, healthcare, and domestic settings
- **Navigate Your Learning Path**: Understand how the 13-week curriculum builds from foundations to complete autonomous systems

## Prerequisites

This is the **first technical chapter**‚Äîno prior robotics knowledge required! You should have:

- **Curiosity** about how AI systems interact with the physical world
- **Openness** to interdisciplinary thinking (AI + physics + engineering + philosophy)
- **Basic programming** familiarity (we'll introduce ROS 2 and robotics code gradually)

*If you have software engineering or AI/ML experience, you'll learn how these skills translate to physical systems. If you're new to both, we'll build from first principles.*
